Quick Start

Three Steps to Control

01

Sideload the APK

Download and install on any Android 8+ device. Enable "Install from unknown sources" in Settings — takes under a minute.

02

Flash the Arduino Sketch

Open SmartHome_CustomPins.ino in Arduino IDE and upload to your UNO, Nano, or Mega.

03

Pair & Start Controlling

Pair HC-05 in Android Bluetooth settings, add it to a Device Slot, tap Connect, and say "Hey Robo".

What's New in v10

9 Flagship Features

Online Hardware Guide

Tap the ℹ icon to open a live WebView of the wiring guide from your Netlify URL — always current without an app update.

WebView

Party Mode Scene

Alternates Left & Right lights every 400ms for 10 seconds. Trigger via the Scenes card or voice — "Hey Robo party mode".

Scene

Master Power FAB

Floating action button turns ALL devices on or off in one tap. Shows green when all off, red when any device is active.

FAB

Motion Gestures

Tilt left → Left Light · right → Right Light · forward → Left Fan · backward → Right Fan. 1.5s cooldown.

Accelerometer

Shake to Toggle All

Shake phone (>2.5g) toggles all devices. Any device on → all off. All off → all on. Slightly longer haptic (100ms) confirms shake detection.

Gesture

Gesture Haptics

Phone vibrates 60ms on tilt detection, 100ms on shake. Physical confirmation with no audio. Can be toggled off in the Settings tab.

Haptic

Biometric App Lock

Locks the whole app on launch. Supports fingerprint, Face ID, and screen PIN/pattern/password via Android BiometricPrompt API.

Security

Lottie Splash Screen

opening.json animation plays with opening.mp3 audio on every launch, then transitions to the lock screen after 4 seconds.

Animation

OTA Update Checker

Fetches SmartController_version.json from Netlify on startup. If remote version > 10, a non-blocking update dialog appears.

OTA

  • ⏱️"left light on for 2 seconds"

  • ⚡"blink left light 3 times"
  • ⚡"flash right light 5 times"

  • 🔇"cancel all timers"

Hey Robo — Wake Word

  • 🤖"hey robo switch to device 1"
  • 🤖"hey robo connect second device"
  • 🤖"hey robo use device 3"
  • 🤖"hey robo disconnect"
  • 🎉"hey robo party mode"
  • 🎉"party mode" — also standalone
The normaliser corrects mishearings: "lift"→"left", "van"→"fan", "awn"→"on", "write"→"right" via Levenshtein distance.

Audio Routing

  • 🔊"speaker 1"
  • 🔊"speaker 2"
  • 🔊"speaker 3"
  • 🔊"speaker 4"

Routes TTS output to any paired Bluetooth A2DP or BLE headset via Android AudioManager.setCommunicationDevice().

Manual Installation

SmartController uses raw Bluetooth SPP — not available on the Play Store. Follow these steps:

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmartController free to use?
Yes — completely free for personal and educational use. The APK, Arduino sketch, and wiring guide are all provided at no cost.
Why does Android show an "unknown sources" warning?
SmartController uses the Bluetooth SPP (Serial Port Profile) API which isn't allowed on the Play Store. Go to Settings → Special App Access → Install Unknown Apps, enable it for your file manager, then install normally. This is safe.
Does voice control require internet?
No. Voice processing uses Android's on-device speech recognition engine. The app works fully offline at runtime. Internet is only used on startup to check for OTA updates and when you open the WebView Hardware Guide.
My HC-05 isn't connecting — what should I check?
First pair the HC-05 in Android's native Bluetooth settings (PIN is usually 1234 or 0000). Then open SmartController → Devices tab → Scan → add to a slot → tap Connect. The app tries the SPP UUID first, then falls back to raw Rfcomm on channel 1 automatically.
How do I customise the Arduino pins?
Download https://joykumbhakar.netlify.app/SmartHome_CustomPins.ino and edit the pin constants at the top. Default: Left Light → 2, Right Light → 3, Left Fan → 4, Right Fan → 5. Re-upload after any changes.
How does the OTA update checker work?
On launch the app silently fetches SmartController_version.json from joykumbhakar.netlify.app. If the JSON's version field exceeds 10, a non-blocking dialog appears with release notes and a direct download link. Network errors are silently ignored.
Can I add more than 4 Bluetooth devices?
Current build supports a maximum of 4 saved device slots. Switch instantly via voice: "Hey Robo switch to device 2". Only one device is active (connected) at a time.
